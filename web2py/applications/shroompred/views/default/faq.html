Frequently Ask Questions

What is ShroomPred?

ShroomPred is a mushroom edibility prediction app.

Based on observable physical features as well as biological properties, the app can predict whether it is edible or poisonous.

I am getting Error or Unknown prediction, is this usual?

The model for predicting the edibility of the mushroom was done based on the mushroom Kaggle competition, and follows the labels found here: {{=A("https://www.kaggle.com/uciml/mushroom-classification", _href="https://www.kaggle.com/uciml/mushroom-classification")}}.

Based on the metadata, there are more label types than found in our training data, therefore, sometimes it produces an Unknown edibility status prediction. The error, in this exercise, also show the unknown status including some other types of error that may occur during submission of the data to the prediction engine.

We are continuously trying to improve our training data and will try to show more meaningful errors in the future.
